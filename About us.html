About us

The Difference Association is a dynamic, progressive and innovative non-profit organisation in the field of intellectual disability, offering diverse services at many levels. All developments and services offered by the organisation are a direct response to the needs identified in the communities.

MISSION

The Difference Centre Association is committed to the development and inclusion of children with disabilities and delays – intellectual, developmental, and physical – in partnership with families and communities

Vision

The Difference Association partners with business organisations and community groups to grow the capacity of emerging projects for children with disabilities/delays. These projects are rolled out over two to three years.
